About sh-miR Designer v2.0

Sh-miR designer is a project we started during iGEM 2013. This year we would like to continue and expand functionality of the software. sh-miR designer v1.0 is aimed to create sh-miR molecules based on siRNAs provided by the user. In sh-miR designer v2.0 only the mRNA number (from NCBI database), which expression should be decreased can be provided.

Moreover, we expanded functionality of the software with off-target validation and check of immune motifs and also extended miRNA-shuttles database. sh-miR Designer is a software aimed for fast and efficient design of effective RNA interference (RNAi) reagents - sh-miRs, also known as artificial miRNAs. sh-miRs are RNA particles whose structure is based on miRNA precursor pri-miRNA, but sequence interacting with transcript is changed depending on research purpose.

Maintenance of structure of pri-miRNA is very important to enable cellular processing and therefore ensure functionality of artificial particles. sh-miRs delivered to cells on genetic vectors - plasmids or viral vectors - enter natural RNAi pathway and silence target mRNA. They can be used in genetic therapies and basic biomedical research. We will provide two applications to access the software, one which require siRNA sequences and the second which require transcript accession number from NCBI database. Each user will receive an account with login (e-mail) and password where he/she would be able to check history of software usage.

Use our website as a client for sh-miR Desiger.

Choose one of two possibilities at the top of the page. Design a sh-miR: from structure (NCBI mRNA number) or from siRNA strand (or two). After filling out the chosen form you will be redirected to the "Design Process" page - unique for the input you have provided. You might see the process is in progress - that is completely OK - it takes some time before it is finished.

Do I have to wait?

If you do not want to wait until designing process is finished, we have something for you. At the end of both from structure and from sirna forms you can type your e-mail and you will be informed about completed task.

Additionally, you can create your very own account. It enables you to save designing history of yours for the future needs. To create account, follow these 4 simple steps:

  1. Go to "sign up" page.
  2. Fill the registration form.
  3. Confirmation code has been sent to your e-mail. Click the link.
  4. Log in.
You are now able to see all processes you have started under the "history" link.
